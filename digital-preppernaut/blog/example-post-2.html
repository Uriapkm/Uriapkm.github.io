Essential Software for Digital Preppers

May 14, 2025 | By: The Digital Preppernaut

A curated collection of open-source tools that every digital prepper should have in their preparedness arsenal.

Offline Document Management

  • LibreOffice - Complete office suite
  • CherryTree - Hierarchical note manager
  • Calibre - eBook library

Security and Privacy

  • VeraCrypt - File encryption
  • KeePassXC - Offline password manager
  • Tor Browser - Anonymous browsing

Communication

  • Signal - Secure messaging
  • Thunderbird - Email client

In future posts, we will analyze each tool in detail and explain how to configure them properly.
