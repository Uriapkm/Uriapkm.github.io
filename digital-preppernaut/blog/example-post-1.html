Basic Guide to Digital Preparedness

May 15, 2025 | By: The Digital Preppernaut

In an increasingly technology-dependent world, digital preparedness has become a fundamental necessity. This guide will introduce you to the basic concepts that every digital prepper should know.

What is Digital Preparedness?

Digital preparedness involves having systems and procedures in place to maintain access to critical information and technological functionality even in situations of disconnection or emergency.

Fundamental Principles

  • Data Redundancy
  • Cloud Independence
  • Information Security
  • Digital Self-Sufficiency
