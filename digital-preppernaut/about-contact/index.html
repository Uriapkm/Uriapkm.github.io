About The Digital Preppernaut

Our Mission

The Digital Preppernaut was born from the need to provide resources and knowledge about digital preparedness. We focus on promoting technological independence, privacy, and digital resilience through the use of free software and secure practices.

Core Values

Independence

We promote digital autonomy and the ability to maintain control over our data.

Transparency

All our content and code is open and verifiable by the community.

Education

We share knowledge to empower others in their digital preparedness.

Community

We build a support network to share resources and experiences.
