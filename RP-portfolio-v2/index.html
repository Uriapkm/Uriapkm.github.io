About Me

Passionate about precision engineering and product development

Mechanical Design Engineer with a Focus on Functional Prototyping

The challenge: Great product ideas often fail due to poor mechanical design, unrealistic tolerances, or designs that can't be manufactured efficiently.

My approach: I combine precision CAD design with hands-on prototyping expertise. Using parametric modeling in FreeCAD, I create designs optimized for manufacturing (DFM/DFA), perform tolerance analysis, and rapidly iterate through physical prototypes using 3D printing and CNC machining.

The outcome: Products that work reliably from day one, reduced development costs, and faster time-to-market. My designs have saved over €8,000 per project on average while improving performance and manufacturability.

With 6+ years of experience developing 50+ functional prototypes and precision mechanisms, I bridge the gap between concept and production-ready designs across diverse industries.

Precision Variable Angle Mechanism

Commercial precision at a fraction of the cost: €8,000+ saved

🎯 The challenge: Researchers needed to measure dynamic contact angles with precision, but commercial equipment cost over €8,000 and didn't allow fine adjustments.

💡 What I did: I designed a precision adapter with a continuously variable angle mechanism using FreeCAD. I combined high-precision 3D printing with machined components to ensure rigidity and measurement repeatability, applying tolerance analysis for optimal fit.

✅ Outcome: Saved over €8,000, achieved greater flexibility than commercial equipment, and delivered more consistent measurements. The mechanism is now routinely used in the lab.
